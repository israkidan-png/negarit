Citable permanent URLs
Every law gets a stable address

Following the Akoma Ntoso (AKN) standard used by Kenya Law and Laws.Africa, every Ethiopian law on Negarit has a structured, permanent URL that lawyers and academics can cite in briefs, papers, and court submissions.

Investment Proclamation No. 1180/2020 — English text:

negarit.et/akn/et/act/2020/1180/eng

Same law, as it stood on 1 Jan 2023 — Amharic:

negarit.et/akn/et/act/2020/1180/amh@2023-01-01

Amendment tracker — live example

The most powerful feature on Negarit — and the one that exists nowhere else in Ethiopia.

Labour Proclamation — Article 34: Maternity Leave Proc. 377/2003 → Proc. 1156/2019 Effective: 7 Oct 2019
Before — Proc. 377/2003
A female worker shall be entitled to 45 days of maternity leave with full pay, of which at least 30 days shall be taken after confinement.
After — Proc. 1156/2019
A female worker shall be entitled to 120 days of maternity leave with full pay, of which at least 60 days shall be taken after confinement.
Federal Negarit Gazette — Vol. 25, No. 37, October 7, 2019 View full amendment history
